SPROUT-LIGHT GROUP TRADING PARTNERSHIP

Location: [Insert Location]

Name: [Your Full Name]

Role: Chief Executive Officer & Capital Provider

PARTY B (Trading Partners):

  • Name: ADANU [Full Name] - Address: [Address] - LEAD TRADER
  • Name: MANSUR [Full Name] - Address: [Address]
  • Name: MOHAMAD [Full Name] - Address: [Address]
  • Name: PAUL [Full Name] - Address: [Address]

PARTY C (Secretary):

Name: [Secretary Full Name]
Address: [Secretary Address]
Role: Group Secretary & Administrative Officer

1. PURPOSE AND OBJECTIVES

This Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) establishes the Sprout-Light Group as a trading partnership with the following objectives:

  • To engage in profitable trading activities using pooled capital
  • To generate sustainable returns for all partners
  • To build capital foundation for future business ventures under the Sprout-Light Group umbrella
  • To operate with transparency, mutual respect, and shared accountability

2. CAPITAL STRUCTURE AND RESOURCES

Initial Trading Capital: USD $1,000 (One Thousand US Dollars)

Capital Provider: Party A provides 100% of initial trading capital

Capital Ownership: All capital remains the property of Party A unless otherwise amended

Operational Resources Provided by Party A:

  • Trading screens/monitors for trading operations
  • Laptops/computers for all active traders
  • Internet connectivity and data subscriptions
  • Electricity bills and power backup systems
  • Trading software subscriptions and tools
  • Office space/co-working space (if applicable)
  • Any other operational expenses necessary for trading activities

3. ROLES AND RESPONSIBILITIES

3.1 Party A (CEO) Responsibilities:

  • Provide initial and ongoing trading capital as agreed
  • Provide all necessary operational resources (equipment, internet, electricity, software)
  • Cover all bills and operational expenses
  • Pay secretary salary from external funds (not from trading profits)
  • Oversee strategic direction of the group
  • Final authority on major decisions
  • Receive monthly reports from Lead Trader
  • Right to audit trading activities at any time

3.2 Lead Trader (ADANU) Responsibilities:

  • Coordinate daily trading activities among all traders
  • Review and approve major trades before execution
  • Provide weekly consolidated reports to Party A
  • Mentor and guide other traders
  • Lead strategy discussions and risk management
  • Ensure all traders follow agreed trading rules
  • Final decision on trade execution during market hours
  • Maintain team discipline and accountability

3.3 Party B (All Traders) Responsibilities:

  • Execute trading strategies with skill and diligence
  • Follow the Lead Trader's coordination and guidance
  • Protect capital and minimize unnecessary risks
  • Maintain confidentiality of trading strategies
  • Properly care for and maintain all equipment provided by Party A
  • Use provided resources exclusively for Sprout-Light Group trading activities
  • Return all equipment in good condition upon exit or termination

3.4 Party C (Secretary) Responsibilities:

  • Maintain accurate records of all trades and transactions
  • Document weekly and monthly trading reports
  • Coordinate meeting schedules and prepare meeting minutes
  • Track profit distributions and payment schedules
  • Maintain equipment inventory and condition reports
  • Handle administrative communications within the group
  • Prepare monthly financial summaries for Party A
  • Maintain confidentiality of all group activities

4. PROFIT DISTRIBUTION

Profit Calculation Period: Monthly (calculated on the last day of each month)

Profit Distribution Formula:

  • Party A (CEO): 38% of monthly profits
  • ADANU (Lead Trader): 12% of monthly profits
  • MANSUR (Trader): 10% of monthly profits
  • MOHAMAD (Trader): 10% of monthly profits
  • PAUL (Trader): 10% of monthly profits
  • Reinvestment Fund (Compounding): 20% of monthly profits

Secretary Compensation:

Party C (Secretary): $100 USD fixed monthly salary, payment begins from Month 7 of operations, paid by Party A from external funds (not from trading profits).

Payment Schedule: Profit distributions shall be made within 7 days of month-end calculations

5. LOSS MANAGEMENT

Loss Responsibility: All trading losses shall be borne by Party A as the capital provider

Loss Limits: Maximum monthly loss threshold should not exceed 15%. If monthly losses exceed threshold, trading must pause for review.

Accountability: Reckless trading or violation of agreed strategies may result in removal from the group

6. TERM AND TERMINATION

Initial Term: This agreement is valid for 12 months from date of signing

Renewal: Automatically renews for additional 12-month periods unless terminated

Voluntary Exit: Must provide 30 days written notice. Entitled to unpaid profit distributions only.

7. CONFIDENTIALITY

All parties agree to maintain strict confidentiality regarding:

  • Trading strategies and methods
  • Financial performance and capital amounts
  • Partner identities and personal information
  • Business plans and future ventures

8. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

Parties agree to first attempt resolution through direct negotiation. If negotiation fails, parties agree to mediation before litigation.

This is a digital copy for reference. Original signed documents are maintained by all parties.
